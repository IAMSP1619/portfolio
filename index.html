And I'm a Fullstack Developer

A full-stack developer is a software developer who has expertise in both the front-end and back-end of a website. The front-end refers to the parts of a website that a user sees and interacts with, while the back-end refers to the behind-the-scenes data storage and processing1. A full-stack developer is responsible for designing, building, and maintaining both the front-end and back-end of a website. This includes developing and maintaining web services and interfaces, contributing to front-end and back-end development processes, building new product features or APIs, performing tests, troubleshooting software, and fixing bugs
